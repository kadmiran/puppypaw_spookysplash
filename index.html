About

Spooky Splash: Subway War is a cute, fast-paced wave-defense game. You are a little ghost guarding a bright voxel subway station — splash the slime monsters with your soul water gun, but never hit the commuters who can't see you. Aim carefully through the crowd, build combos, and grow stronger every level!

Features

  • 👻 First-person water-shooting wave defense
  • 🌊 Endless waves with 10+ monster types and a mini-boss
  • ⭐ Roguelite perks, combo & fever scoring
  • 📱 Simple touch controls — joystick + drag to aim
  • ✈️ Fully offline · free to play · no account needed

Available on iPhone. (App Store link coming soon.)

소개
